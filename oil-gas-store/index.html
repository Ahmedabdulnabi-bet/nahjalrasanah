How We Work with Oil & Gas Clients

  1. Technical inquiry / RFQ: Client sends bill of quantities, datasheets or specification references using the RFQ basket or official tender request.
  2. Clarification & alignment: We verify scope, standards (API, ASME, IEC, EN), material classes and documentation requirements.
  3. Sourcing & evaluation: We approach approved OEMs and regional stockists, checking lead time, warranty, certificates and compliance.
  4. Offer preparation: Technical and commercial offers are prepared in line with the client's tender format and instructions.
  5. Execution & after-sales: Once awarded, we coordinate manufacturing, inspection, logistics, documentation and after-sales support.

For complex tenders, this online catalog can be complemented with detailed Excel BOQs, manufacturer datasheets and compliance tables prepared specifically for each project.
