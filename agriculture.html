About Our Agricultural Division

At Nahj Al-Rasanah, we believe agriculture is the backbone of a prosperous society. Our agricultural investment arm focuses on transforming arid lands into vibrant, high-yield farms using modern greenhouse technology, precision irrigation, and data-driven crop management.

Key Pillars

Innovation

Incorporating IoT sensors, AI monitoring, and mobile irrigation systems to maximize efficiency and sustainability.

Localization

Employing local talent and empowering Iraqi farmers through tools and training.

Crop Selection

Focusing on high-demand and climate-resilient crops suitable for Iraq’s geography and market.
